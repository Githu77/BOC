Emergency Protocols

Know what to do in case of an emergency

01

Recognize the Emergency

Identify life-threatening situations such as chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe bleeding, loss of consciousness, or signs of stroke.

02

Call for Help

Dial our emergency hotline at +254(0) 723 043 404 or the national emergency number. Clearly state your location and the nature of the emergency.

03

Provide First Aid

If trained, administer appropriate first aid while waiting for emergency services to arrive. Follow instructions provided by emergency dispatchers.

04

Prepare for Transport

Gather essential items like ID, insurance information, and a list of current medications if possible, without delaying emergency care.

In case of a life-threatening emergency, always call for professional help immediately. Do not attempt to transport a critically ill person yourself unless absolutely necessary.

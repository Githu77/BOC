Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting and visiting Brooks of Cherith Hospitals.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment line at +254(0) 723 043 405, emailing appointments@brookoflife.co.ke, or using our online appointment booking system on our website. For specialist consultations, we recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance.

What are your visiting hours?

Our visiting hours for inpatient wards are from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM daily. We kindly ask that visitors respect these hours to ensure our patients get adequate rest. Special arrangements can be made for critical cases by speaking with the nurse in charge.

How can I access my medical records?

You can request access to your medical records by filling out a Medical Records Request Form at our reception or downloading it from our website and submitting it to records@brookoflife.co.ke. Please note that we require proper identification to protect your privacy. Records are typically processed within 3-5 business days.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, our Main Hospital in Westlands offers 24/7 emergency services. Our emergency department is equipped to handle all types of medical emergencies, with specialized trauma teams available around the clock. For emergencies, please call +254(0) 723 043 404 or come directly to our emergency entrance.

How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We value your feedback and continuously strive to improve our services. You can provide feedback by filling out our patient satisfaction survey available at all our facilities, sending an email to feedback@brookoflife.co.ke, or using the contact form on this page. All feedback is reviewed by our quality improvement team.

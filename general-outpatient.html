Comprehensive Primary Healthcare

At Brooks of Cherith Hospitals, our General Outpatient Services provide comprehensive healthcare for patients of all ages. From routine check-ups to managing chronic conditions, our team of healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing personalized care that addresses your unique health needs.

We believe in a holistic approach to healthcare, focusing not just on treating illness but also on preventive care and health education. Our general outpatient department serves as your first point of contact for all healthcare needs, providing continuity of care and coordinating with specialists when necessary.

Our General Outpatient Services

Explore our comprehensive range of primary healthcare services

Primary Care

Our primary care services focus on providing comprehensive healthcare for patients of all ages. We offer routine check-ups, preventive care, and management of common illnesses to help you maintain optimal health and well-being.

Services Offered:

  • Routine physical examinations
  • Health screenings and assessments
  • Immunizations and vaccinations
  • Management of common illnesses
  • Health education and counseling
  • Referrals to specialists when needed

Chronic Disease Management

Our chronic disease management program provides ongoing care and support for patients with long-term health conditions. We work with you to develop personalized treatment plans that help you effectively manage your condition and improve your quality of life.

Services Offered:

  • Diabetes management
  • Hypertension (high blood pressure) care
  • Asthma and COPD management
  • Heart disease monitoring
  • Medication management
  • Lifestyle modification guidance
  • Regular monitoring and follow-up

Antenatal Care

Our antenatal care services provide comprehensive prenatal care for expectant mothers throughout pregnancy. We offer regular check-ups, screenings, and education to ensure the health and well-being of both mother and baby.

Services Offered:

  • Prenatal check-ups and monitoring
  • Ultrasound services
  • Maternal nutrition guidance
  • Prenatal vitamins and supplements
  • Childbirth education
  • High-risk pregnancy management
  • Postnatal care and support

Preventive Care

Our preventive care services focus on keeping you healthy and preventing illness before it occurs. We offer a range of screenings, vaccinations, and health education to help you maintain optimal health and detect potential health issues early.

Services Offered:

  • Health risk assessments
  • Cancer screenings
  • Cardiovascular disease screenings
  • Adult immunizations
  • Lifestyle counseling
  • Nutritional guidance
  • Weight management

General Physician

Our general physicians are the first point of contact for most health concerns. They provide comprehensive care for a wide range of conditions and can refer you to specialists when needed. Our physicians focus on building long-term relationships with patients to provide continuity of care.

Services Offered:

  • Diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses
  • Management of acute and chronic conditions
  • Preventive healthcare and screenings
  • Health risk assessments
  • Referrals to specialists when needed
  • Follow-up care after hospitalization
  • Coordination of care across multiple providers

Family Planning Clinic

Our Family Planning Clinic provides confidential counseling and a range of contraceptive options to help individuals and couples make informed decisions about their reproductive health. We offer personalized care that respects your values, preferences, and health needs.

Services Offered:

  • Comprehensive contraceptive counseling
  • Short-term contraceptive methods (pills, patches, rings)
  • Long-acting reversible contraceptives (IUDs, implants)
  • Barrier methods (condoms, diaphragms)
  • Natural family planning education
  • Emergency contraception
  • Pre-conception counseling
  • STI screening and prevention

Well Baby Clinic

Our Well Baby Clinic provides comprehensive healthcare for infants and young children, focusing on growth monitoring, developmental assessments, immunizations, and parental education. We partner with parents to ensure the optimal health and development of their children.

Services Offered:

  • Regular growth and development monitoring
  • Comprehensive immunization services
  • Nutritional assessment and counseling
  • Developmental milestone screening
  • Early detection of health issues
  • Breastfeeding support and guidance
  • Parenting education and support
  • Management of common childhood illnesses

Physiotherapy

Our Physiotherapy services help patients recover movement and function after injury, illness, or disability. Using evidence-based techniques and personalized treatment plans, our physiotherapists work with you to improve mobility, reduce pain, and enhance your quality of life.

Services Offered:

  • Musculoskeletal assessment and treatment
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Sports injury rehabilitation
  • Back and neck pain management
  • Neurological rehabilitation
  • Pediatric physiotherapy
  • Geriatric physiotherapy
  • Ergonomic assessment and advice
  • Exercise prescription and guidance

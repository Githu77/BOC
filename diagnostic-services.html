Advanced Diagnostic Solutions

At Brooks of Cherith Hospitals, our Diagnostic Services department is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by experienced professionals to provide accurate and timely diagnostic results. We offer a comprehensive range of diagnostic tests and procedures to help identify and monitor various medical conditions.

Our diagnostic center plays a crucial role in the healthcare journey, providing physicians with the information they need to make informed decisions about your treatment. We are committed to delivering precise results with minimal wait times, ensuring that you receive the care you need as quickly as possible.

Why Choose Our Diagnostic Services

  • Advanced Technology: State-of-the-art equipment for accurate and detailed results
  • Expert Technicians: Highly trained professionals with specialized expertise
  • Quick Turnaround: Efficient processes to deliver results promptly
  • Safety Protocols: Strict adherence to safety and infection control standards
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Detailed reports with clear interpretations

Imaging Services

Our imaging services utilize advanced technology to provide detailed images of the body's internal structures. These images help physicians diagnose and monitor various medical conditions, from broken bones to complex diseases.

Services Offered:

  • X-ray imaging
  • Ultrasound
  • Computed Tomography (CT) scans
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Mammography
  • Bone density scans
  • Fluoroscopy
  • Interventional radiology

Laboratory Services

Our laboratory services provide comprehensive testing of blood, urine, and other body fluids and tissues to help diagnose, treat, and prevent disease. Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with advanced technology and staffed by experienced professionals.

Services Offered:

  • Complete blood count (CBC)
  • Blood chemistry panels
  • Urinalysis
  • Microbiology cultures
  • Pathology services
  • Immunology testing
  • Hormone testing
  • Genetic testing

Cardiac Diagnostics

Our cardiac diagnostic services provide comprehensive evaluation of heart function to help diagnose and monitor cardiovascular conditions. We use advanced technology to assess heart health and detect potential issues early.

Services Offered:

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG)
  • Echocardiogram
  • Stress testing
  • Holter monitoring
  • Event monitoring
  • Cardiac CT scans
  • Cardiac MRI
  • Cardiac catheterization

Neurological Diagnostics

Our neurological diagnostic services provide comprehensive evaluation of the nervous system to help diagnose and monitor neurological conditions. We use advanced technology to assess brain and nerve function.

Services Offered:

  • Electroencephalogram (EEG)
  • Electromyography (EMG)
  • Nerve conduction studies
  • Brain CT scans
  • Brain MRI
  • Lumbar puncture
  • Cognitive testing
  • Sleep studies

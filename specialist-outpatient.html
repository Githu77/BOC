Comprehensive Specialist Care

At Brooks of Cherith Hospitals, our Specialist Outpatient Services provide expert care from experienced medical professionals in various specialized fields of medicine. We offer consultations, treatments, and follow-up care for complex medical conditions, ensuring that you receive the highest quality of specialized healthcare.

Our team of specialists works collaboratively to provide integrated care, ensuring that all aspects of your health are addressed. Whether you need a one-time consultation or ongoing specialized care, our experts are dedicated to improving your health outcomes with personalized treatment plans.

Our Medical Specialties

Explore our range of specialized medical services provided by expert healthcare professionals

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive care for heart and cardiovascular conditions. Our experienced cardiologists use advanced diagnostic tools and treatment approaches to diagnose and manage a wide range of heart conditions.

Services Offered:

  • Comprehensive cardiac evaluation
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG)
  • Echocardiography
  • Stress testing
  • Holter monitoring
  • Hypertension management
  • Heart failure management
  • Coronary artery disease treatment

Neurology

Our neurology department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our neurologists provide expert care for a wide range of neurological conditions.

Services Offered:

  • Neurological evaluation
  • Electroencephalogram (EEG)
  • Electromyography (EMG)
  • Headache and migraine management
  • Epilepsy management
  • Stroke prevention and care
  • Movement disorders treatment
  • Neurodegenerative disease management

Rheumatology

Our rheumatology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of arthritis, autoimmune diseases, and other disorders affecting the joints, muscles, and bones. Our rheumatologists provide comprehensive care for patients with rheumatic diseases.

Services Offered:

  • Rheumatological evaluation
  • Autoimmune disease management
  • Rheumatoid arthritis treatment
  • Osteoarthritis management
  • Lupus treatment
  • Gout management
  • Joint injections
  • Chronic pain management

Pulmonology

Our pulmonology department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of respiratory conditions and lung diseases. Our pulmonologists provide expert care for patients with acute and chronic respiratory disorders.

Services Offered:

  • Pulmonary function testing
  • Asthma management
  • COPD treatment
  • Sleep apnea evaluation
  • Bronchoscopy
  • Lung cancer screening
  • Pulmonary rehabilitation
  • Respiratory infection treatment

Physician Consultant

Our Physician Consultant service provides comprehensive medical evaluations and management for a wide range of health conditions. Our experienced physicians offer personalized care and coordinate with specialists when needed.

Services Offered:

  • Comprehensive medical evaluations
  • Management of chronic diseases
  • Preventive healthcare
  • Health risk assessments
  • Specialist referrals and coordination
  • Follow-up care
  • Medical advice and education
  • Medication management

Wound Clinic

Our Wound Clinic specializes in the assessment, treatment, and management of acute and chronic wounds. We provide evidence-based care to promote optimal healing and prevent complications.

Services Offered:

  • Comprehensive wound assessment
  • Diabetic foot ulcer management
  • Pressure ulcer treatment
  • Venous and arterial ulcer care
  • Surgical wound management
  • Advanced wound dressings
  • Wound debridement
  • Patient education for wound care

Nutritionist Counselling

Our Nutritionist Counselling service provides personalized dietary advice and nutrition plans to help you achieve optimal health. Our registered nutritionists work with you to develop sustainable eating habits that support your health goals.

Services Offered:

  • Nutritional assessment and evaluation
  • Personalized meal planning
  • Weight management counseling
  • Diabetes nutrition management
  • Heart-healthy diet planning
  • Sports nutrition
  • Nutrition for chronic disease management
  • Food allergy and intolerance guidance

Services Offered:

  • Comprehensive blood tests
  • Cardiac evaluation
  • Pulmonary function testing
  • Vision and hearing assessment
  • Cancer screening
  • Personalized health risk assessment

Obs/Gynae Clinic

Our Obstetrics and Gynecology Clinic provides comprehensive care for women's reproductive health. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, our experienced gynecologists offer personalized care throughout all stages of a woman's life.

Services Offered:

  • Prenatal care and delivery
  • Gynecological examinations
  • Pap smears and cervical cancer screening
  • Family planning and contraception
  • Menopause management
  • Treatment for gynecological conditions
  • Gynecological surgeries
  • High-risk pregnancy management

Hypertension Clinic

Our Hypertension Clinic specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of high blood pressure. Our team provides comprehensive care to help you control your blood pressure and reduce the risk of complications.

Services Offered:

  • Blood pressure assessment and monitoring
  • Personalized treatment plans
  • Medication management
  • Lifestyle modification counseling
  • Risk factor assessment
  • Home blood pressure monitoring guidance
  • Management of resistant hypertension
  • Prevention of hypertension-related complications

Services Offered:

  • Pulmonary function testing

  • COPD treatment

  • Sleep-disordered breathing assessment

Services Offered:

  • Fracture management

  • Sports injury treatment
  • Arthroscopic procedures
  • Spine disorders management
  • Pediatric orthopedics
  • Orthopedic rehabilitation

Urologist & Prostate Clinic

Services Offered:

  • Comprehensive urological evaluation
  • Prostate cancer screening and treatment
  • Benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) management
  • Urinary tract infection treatment
  • Kidney stone management
  • Erectile dysfunction treatment
  • Male infertility evaluation
  • Urological surgeries

Dental Clinic

Our Dental Clinic provides comprehensive dental care for patients of all ages. From preventive care to restorative and cosmetic procedures, our dental team is committed to helping you maintain optimal oral health.

  • Comprehensive dental examinations
  • Professional teeth cleaning
  • Fillings and restorations
  • Root canal therapy

Diabetic Clinic

Our Diabetic Clinic specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of diabetes. Our team provides comprehensive care to help you manage your diabetes effectively and prevent complications.

Services Offered:

  • Personalized diabetes management plans

  • Prevention and management of diabetic complications

  • Fertility counseling and support

Services Available at This Location

Our Ol Jororok Branch offers essential healthcare services to the community

General Outpatient

Comprehensive primary healthcare services for patients of all ages, from routine check-ups to chronic disease management.

Learn More

Preventive Care

Preventive health services including vaccinations, health screenings, and wellness check-ups to help maintain optimal health.

Learn More

Basic Laboratory Services

Essential laboratory tests including blood work, urinalysis, and other routine diagnostic tests to aid in diagnosis and treatment.

Learn More

Pharmacy Services

On-site pharmacy providing prescribed medications and over-the-counter products with professional pharmacist consultation.

Learn More

Maternal & Child Health

Basic maternal and child health services including antenatal care, well-baby check-ups, and childhood vaccinations.

Learn More

Minor Procedures

Basic medical procedures including wound care, suturing, and other minor treatments that don't require hospitalization.

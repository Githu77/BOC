About Brooks of Cherith

We are a faith-driven medical foundation committed to providing compassionate, quality healthcare to our community since 2015.

Our Mission

"To promote ideal treatment and prevention of illness through unique, quality and effective personalized services."

At Brooks of Cherith Hospitals, we believe in treating the whole person—body, mind, and spirit—with dignity and respect. Our mission guides everything we do, from the way we interact with patients to the services we provide and the facilities we build.

We are committed to:

  • Providing accessible, high-quality healthcare to all
  • Treating each patient with compassion and dignity
  • Integrating faith and medical expertise in our approach to healing
  • Continuously improving our services through innovation and education
  • Serving our community beyond the walls of our facilities
Our healthcare professionals embody our mission of compassionate care

Our Journey

Since our founding in 2015, Brooks of Cherith Hospitals has grown from a small clinic to a comprehensive healthcare provider serving thousands of patients annually.

2015

Foundation Established

Brooks of Cherith Hospitals was established by Dr. Samuel Mwangi with a small clinic in Westlands, Nairobi, serving approximately 20 patients per day.

2017

Expansion of Services

Added specialist outpatient services, including cardiology, neurology, and rheumatology departments, expanding our ability to provide comprehensive care.

2019

Main Hospital Opening

Opened our current main hospital facility in Westlands, featuring state-of-the-art equipment, 50 inpatient beds, and 24/7 emergency services.

2021

Rural Outreach

Established our first satellite clinic in Ol Jororok, Nyandarua County, bringing essential healthcare services to rural communities.

2023

Advanced Diagnostic Center

Launched our comprehensive diagnostic center with advanced imaging technology, including MRI, CT scan, and specialized laboratory services.

2025

Today & Beyond

Today, we serve over 5,000 patients annually across our facilities, with plans to expand our services and reach even more communities in need.
